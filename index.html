About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters in a constantly evolving battlefield. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters and collecting power-ups to become stronger.

With each level up, you'll gain new abilities and weapons to combat the increasingly difficult hordes of enemies. Strategic decision-making and quick reflexes are key to mastering this challenging game.

Inspired by popular roguelike titles but with its own unique twist, Monster Survivors offers hours of addictive gameplay that will keep you coming back for "just one more run".

Survival Tips

  1. Keep Moving

    Standing still is a death sentence. Develop patterns of movement that allow you to kite enemies effectively.

  2. Prioritize Upgrades

    Focus on upgrading a few powerful weapons rather than spreading your upgrades across many different ones.

  3. Collect Experience Gems

    Don't let experience gems go to waste. Make sure to collect them before they disappear.

  4. Balance Offensive and Defensive Items

    Having powerful weapons is important, but don't neglect defensive items that increase your health, armor, or recovery.

  5. Learn Enemy Patterns

    Different monsters have different attack patterns and speeds. Learning these will help you anticipate and avoid danger.
